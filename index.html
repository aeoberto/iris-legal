Privacy Policy – IRIS Display

Effective Date: 2026

IRIS Display respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Information Collection

IRIS Display does not collect personal information, location data, analytics, or user identifiers. The app only communicates with authorized IRIS hardware devices to update display status.

Data Usage

All communication is used solely to control and update IRIS display devices. No data is transmitted to third-party services.

Third-Party Services

IRIS Display does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Security

Communication between the app and IRIS devices is designed to be secure and limited to authorized devices.
